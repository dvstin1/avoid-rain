# Lore Fragments: The Truncated Archive

This document archives the narrative snippets found throughout the Scriptorium. These fragments are manifestations of the Author's original intent, now shattered by The Bleed.

## 1. The Half-Cleft Manuscript
**Fragment ID:** `half_cleft_manuscript`
**Location:** Chapter 1 - Entrance Corridor
**Text:**
> "The quill was never meant to be a weapon. It was a tool of preservation. But when the ink began to boil, we had no choice but to sharpen the nibs. Half the world is now Cleft, separated by a margin that no longer holds its shape. If you are reading this, you are the last Editor. Please... finish the sentence we could not."

## 2. Technical Metadata
- **Standard Dimensions:** All Lore Lecterns ('L') are 1x1 tile entities.
- **Interaction:** [SPACE] key triggers a STANDARD dialogue modal.
- **Persistence:** Collected fragments are logged to the `discovered_lore` dictionary in the player's profile metrics.
