# Idea

I would like in the map editor to be able to place a connected set of markers for npc or boss or enemy placement and they will spawn in them in the top most row, left most entry first to always have the same behaviour. The multiple entries in the map for the npc placement are for them to have a path they will walk. I want the following abilities for each placement: normal, slow, fast, pause 0.5s, pause 3s, and stop. So if a walking NPC or enemy reaches one of these they will adjust their speed accordingly. The base walking speed of different enemies might be different so that should be a property of the enemy and not a map property, but they will all need speed adjustments. The pauses are to make more realistic looking movements for enemies and NPCs. This design decision also means enemies will not cross paths which avoids issues of enemies walking into each other. Also when an NPC or enemy engages in walking they should always adjust their speed by a tiny percentage to make it less predictable. Certain enemies can be slower type and their max speed is normal or slow.
