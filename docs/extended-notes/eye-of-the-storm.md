1. The Lore: "The Eye of the Storm"

    During the Fight: Bosses are massive concentrations of raw text and energy. Their presence bends the local reality, warping the environment into an "Eye of the Storm." The rain halts because all the ambient ink on the page is being actively drawn into the boss entity to fuel its combat form.

    If You Win: The compressed ink shatters. With its malicious frequency broken, the text dilutes instantly, exploding outward as a beautiful downpour of Clear Rain that washes the arena clean.

    If You Lose: Your defense drops. The boss instantly releases its grip on the gathered ink, allowing The Bleed to instantly crash back down over the entire arena, suffocating and erasing your character into a Game Over.
