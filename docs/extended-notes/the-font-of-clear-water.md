The Lore: "The Font of Clear Water"

    The Lore: The fountain is known as The Wellspring. It sits at the absolute center of the Scriptorium. It doesn't draw from water tables in the earth; it is an active artesian pressure valve connected directly to the original, untainted source of the universe—the Unwritten Page.

    The Purification: Before a Reader steps into the Chronicle, they dip their hands or weapons into the fountain to steel their nerves. After a brutal defeat where you are dragged back to the hub covered in monochrome bleaching and psychic ink stains, standing near the fountain washes the residue away. The clear water is the literal proof that the corruption has not yet reached the Sanctuary.

    The Reflection (Stats & Bestiary): When the player peers into the perfectly still, glass-like surface of the basin, it doesn't just show their face—it acts as a liquid mirror that reflects the history of the manuscript. Because water holds memory in this universe, looking deep into the pool allows you to see the shapes of the "Censors" you have encountered (The Bestiary) and the mathematical exactness of your timeline (Wins, Losses, Deaths, and Quit-outs).
