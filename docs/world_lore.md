# World Lore & Structure

## The Shifting World
The game world is a large, fragmented map composed of modular sections. Some sections are static, while others randomly swap between several variants at the start of each run, ensuring no two games are identical.

## The Cycle of Rain
- **Day 1:** The world is consumed by a lethal rain that moves in from the edges. Survival depends on reaching the eye of the storm (the safe circle).
- **Day 2:** The rain vanishes, leaving a scarred landscape. This is the final window for exploration and power-up collection.
- **The End:** As Day 2 closes, a portal opens, leading to the realm of the architect behind the storm.

## The Narrative
The player is a wanderer in a world that is literally dissolving. The "Rain" isn't just water; it's a force of erasure. To stop the cycle, the source—the Final Boss—must be defeated in their own domain.
